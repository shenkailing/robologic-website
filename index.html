Redefining Turf Management with Intelligent Automation

Robologic is a cutting-edge technology company dedicated to transforming landscape maintenance for the modern golf course. We provide intelligent, automated equipment rental solutions that empower clubs to achieve pristine playing conditions while significantly reducing operational costs and labor dependencies.

Tired of the constant challenge of managing labor, fuel, and maintenance costs? Our integrated robotic systems offer a seamless, sustainable, and smarter alternative. By leveraging advanced technologies like AI and GPS, we help you deliver an unparalleled course experience, round after round.

RMS – Robotic Mowing System

The RMS (Robotic Mowing System) is a smart, customizable automation kit that transforms your club’s existing mowers into a sophisticated, self-operating fleet. This cost-effective leasing solution delivers the future of unmanned mowing without the high upfront cost of all-new equipment.

Experience a new standard in efficiency and consistency. The RMS works tirelessly through day, night, and inclement weather, ensuring perfectly mowed fairways on schedule, every time. By automating the process, you not only achieve a superior aesthetic result but also realize significant savings on fuel consumption compared to traditional manual operation.

FJD Z42P Smart Lawn Mower

The FJD Z42P is a professional mowing robot designed for golf driving ranges and turf farms, combining efficient mowing and ball picking in one robot. Equipped with advanced navigation technology, it enables precise unmanned mowing and fast ball collection, significantly reducing labor costs and boosting efficiency. Paired with the FJD laser kit, it can operate smoothly even in RTK-challenging areas like under trees. The Z42P offers a smart and cost-effective solution for professional turf management, making lawn care easier and more efficient.

FJD Robotic Line Marker

Start a new era of autonomous line marking on the sports fields with the AI-powered FJD RLM01! This robotic line marker delivers centimeter-level accuracy through dual industrial-grade GNSS receivers and a local base station. Paired with FJD’s laser kit, it can also provide millimeter-level marking accuracy even in GPS-challenging areas, such as under trees or near spectator stands. The Line Marker can save hundreds of hours and tons of labor costs every year, making it a top pick for marking clear, crispy lines in various sports fields.
With the Robotic Line Marker, your field marking work becomes more efficient and accurate, ensuring that your sports fields are in great condition for each match.
